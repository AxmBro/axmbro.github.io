Better Bedrock

The Better Bedrock is project of Texture Pack for Minecraft Bedrock Edition, Website and Mobile App available only for Android. The Main goal is to improve the default Minecraft gameplay to a whole new level with many new features!

All content here can be downloaded from our website, betterbedrock.com! Only limits are device support for Mobile App (only for Android) and Client (only for Windows, MacOS) You can always rate our work and send possible ideas on our discord, discord.gg/wJhH86c2wb!

Creators

• Texture Pack: Axmbro

• Mobile App & Website & Client: iDarkQ

Technology

• Texture Pack: JSON, Minecraft Bedrock JsonUI

• Mobile App & Website: Flutter, Supabase

• Client: Flutter, Windows API
